# Audit Scope

- Product: Potion Rogue, repository at `C:\Potion Rogue Sort Puzzle RPG`.
- Surfaces: main menu, kit selection, procedural dungeon map, battle HUD and potion board, events, rewards, workshop/settings, victory/defeat flow.
- Primary user: portrait-phone player who wants a readable offline puzzle RPG with short sessions and meaningful long-term progression.
- Primary task: start or continue a run, make route and potion decisions, defeat a boss, and progress to new authored areas.
- Current constraint: one seven-floor Shadow Crypt run currently ends the complete content loop.
- Brand constraints: dark fantasy alchemy, gold/obsidian ornament, Cinzel display typography, illustrated monsters, no network dependency.
- Technical constraints: Godot 4.7.1, GDScript, Android portrait, deterministic procedural generation, local versioned saves, compatibility renderer, no new third-party runtime dependencies.
- Reference bar: the Play Store concept screenshots supplied in this task, especially their dense but legible HUD, authored area identity, premium character art, and clear progression hierarchy.
- Audit objective: identify the ten highest-leverage engine, feature, and visual improvements while preserving the working puzzle/battle core.
